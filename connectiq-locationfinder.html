CONNECT IQ LOCATION FINDER

Location Finder is an application for the Connect IQ app marketplace. It uses your current GPS location and your phones internet connection to find locations of interest near you, all on your wrist!

All locations and directions are provided by the Google Places API and the Google Maps API.

Download at Connect IQ

CURRENT FEATURES

  • Metric Support
  • Turn-by-turn Directions
  • Search by location type
  • Vibrate when data is received
  • Tap to cycle between locations near you
  • Fenix3 and VivoActive Layout

UPCOMING FEATURES

  • "Turn-by-turn" Directions
  • Free text search
  • Directional arrow Directions

Privacy

The Location Finder Connect IQ Application is created and maintained by Austin Green (austingreenkansas@gmail.com). The given application collects anonymized GPS data as provided by the Garmin wearables and provided to the Location Finder webservice. This anonymization reduces the accuracy of the GPS location to 500 meters. I do NOT collect any personal data such as name or email.

If you have any questions about privacy or the information this application collects, please email me at austingreenkansas@gmail.com.
